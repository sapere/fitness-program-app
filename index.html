Full-body 3×/week
Each muscle hit 3× weekly. Meta-analyses show equal or better hypertrophy vs splits when volume is equated (Schoenfeld et al., 2016).

Volume and load increase each week. The primary driver of long-term muscle and strength gain.
Rep range 8–15
Hypertrophy occurs across 6–30 reps when sets approach failure. 8–15 is joint-friendly for your training age.
Deload every 6 weeks
Reduces accumulated fatigue, restores anabolic sensitivity. Especially important over 40 for joints and CNS.
